Elven Prince of Mirkwood

Legolas Greenleaf is a Sindar Elf and the son of Thranduil, King of the Woodland Realm of Mirkwood. As a member of the Fellowship of the Ring, he played a crucial role in the quest to destroy the One Ring.

Known for his exceptional archery skills and keen elven senses, Legolas is a master of the bow and arrow. His eyesight is so sharp that he can see for leagues, and his hearing allows him to detect sounds imperceptible to mortals. He moves with the grace and agility characteristic of his elven heritage.

Throughout the War of the Ring, Legolas formed a close friendship with Gimli the Dwarf, despite the historical animosity between their peoples. Their bond became legendary, symbolizing unity and friendship across different races of Middle-earth.
